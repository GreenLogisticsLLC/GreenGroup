Heavy haul transportation: coordinating oversized equipment with precision

Another successful specialized shipment by Green Logistics LLC

May 6, 2026 Heavy haul · Lowboy

Transporting oversized heavy equipment requires far more than simply moving freight from one location to another. Every load demands detailed planning, experienced coordination, specialized trailers, and strict attention to safety regulations.

Our latest project involved the successful transportation of a heavy-duty dump truck using a specialized lowboy trailer designed for oversized and overweight freight.

Specialized freight requires specialized logistics

This shipment presented several operational challenges due to:

  • the size and weight of the equipment,
  • multiple axle configurations,
  • loading and securement requirements,
  • and route coordination for safe transit.

Heavy equipment transportation demands experienced logistics management to ensure compliance, safety, and timely delivery.

The importance of proper equipment

For this move, a specialized lowboy trailer was utilized to safely transport the dump truck while maintaining stability and weight distribution throughout transit.

At Green Logistics LLC, selecting the correct equipment for each shipment is one of the most important parts of the planning process. Oversized freight often requires:

  • specialized trailers,
  • experienced heavy-haul carriers,
  • permit coordination,
  • and route analysis.

Safety and securement

Safety remains our top priority on every shipment. Proper securement procedures were used to ensure the equipment remained stable during transportation across varying road conditions and terrain.

Our logistics team coordinated directly with the carrier to monitor the shipment and maintain communication throughout the transportation process.
