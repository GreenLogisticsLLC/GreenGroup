Adele Williams

Account Manager

Adele Williams is a highly organized and detail-oriented Account Manager at Green Logistics LLC with strong experience in logistics operations, transportation documentation, and client account coordination. She plays an important role in supporting daily freight operations and ensuring that all transportation processes are handled accurately and efficiently.

At Green Logistics LLC, Adele is responsible for managing and processing essential shipping documentation, including PODs (Proof of Delivery), BOLs (Bills of Lading), Rate Confirmations (RateCons), and other operational paperwork. She also maintains and updates the company’s client database, ensuring accurate records and smooth communication between customers, carriers, and internal departments.

In addition to account management responsibilities, Adele works closely with factoring companies to support invoice processing, payment verification, and financial coordination. She is also involved in coordinating carrier payments and helping ensure timely and accurate settlements for transportation services.

Known for her professionalism, reliability, and strong communication skills, Adele consistently demonstrates the ability to manage multiple responsibilities in a fast-paced logistics environment. Her attention to detail, organizational abilities, and commitment to excellent service make her a valuable member of the Green Logistics LLC team.

Through her dedication and operational expertise, Adele contributes to the company’s reputation for dependable logistics support and high-quality customer service across the transportation industry.
